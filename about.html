About myself

Hi friends! My name is Daniel McKinnon, my friends call me Dan. I am a professional photographer from New York. I hope you enjoyed my work. As always, I am ready for new discoveries in my case, so let's create something new together, it's a be great experience!

In short, my parents instilled a love of photography when I was about 14 years old. Honestly, at first, the photography seemed boring, so many identical shots, and wait for the perfect moment, choose the right lighting... But it turned out that just in these details and hid the miracles of photography. Paying attention to the details, gaining experience, I loved the photo like never before.

Now I want to share my skills with you and to forever preserve the precious moments of life.
